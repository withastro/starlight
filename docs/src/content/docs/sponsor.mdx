## Community maintained

Starlight is an entirely community-maintained solution for high-quality, low-footprint documentation that is perfect for developer docs.
With additional integrations, Starlight can power your entire web presence, including marketing pages and a blog!

## Sponsorship

Sponsoring Starlight is a great way to support the project and help us continue to build and maintain it.
Your financial contributions support [the community behind the open source project](#community-maintained) for ongoing maintenance and development.

<LinkCard
	title="Sponsor Starlight"
	description="Visit Open Collective and choose a custom amount."
	href="https://opencollective.com/astrodotbuild"
/>

We recognize some levels of sponsorship with a logo and/or link on the [Starlight website](/) and [GitHub README](https://github.com/withastro/starlight/blob/main/packages/starlight/README.md).
We are always happy to work directly with larger sponsors ($2000+ per month) to create a custom sponsorship package.

### Astro

Starlight is an entirely community-maintained solution built on top of the [Astro](https://astro.build) framework.
Supporting Astro is a great way to support Starlight, as well as the broader ecosystem of tools and integrations built on top of Astro.

### Development

Plugins can customize Starlight configuration, UI, and behavior, while also being easy to share and reuse.

**[Creating a Starlight plugin](https://starlight.astro.build/resources/plugins/) for your product or service is a great way to make it easy for people to use you in their documentation, and grows the entire ecosystem!**

Either if Starlight is missing a core feature that would allow you to use it, or if you don’t have the time or expertise to build it yourself, partner with us to fund development of a plugin for your product or service.
